Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) is a type of software used by organizations to manage and integrate crucial business processes in one unified system. ERP systems streamline functions like accounting, human resources, inventory management, sales, and procurement, allowing real-time data sharing across departments. This integrated approach improves efficiency, decision-making, and productivity by centralizing data, reducing manual work, and enhancing collaboration within the organization.
